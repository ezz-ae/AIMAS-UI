Vocabulary
----------
Intent (L0): Raw declaration, ephemeral, never stored.
Normalization: Process that converts intent into capsule-ready features.
Intent Capsule (L1): Derived feature object with opaque identity.
Fit Matrix: Deterministic response describing ETA, probability, confidence, sensitivity, and paths.
Free Baseline Path: Mandatory zero-cost route inside every Fit Matrix.
Fairness Gate: Structural enforcement preventing unfair outputs.
Observation Surface: AIMAS-UI pages (docs, search, dashboard) that render protocol truth without altering it.
